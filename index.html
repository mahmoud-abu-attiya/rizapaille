RIZPAILLE BY HDR®

The best solution for everybody! RIZPAILLE by HDR® was inspired by the leaves that surround spring rolls and “white” Asian noodles that are traditionally made of rice dough. Without any treatment, RIZPAILLE by HDR® undergoes no modification of its initial properties. It has an approximate duration of 30 to 45 minutes in any drink, at room temperature or cold and around 10 minutes in a very hot drink such as Irish Coffee for example.
